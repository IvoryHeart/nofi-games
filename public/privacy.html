Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

The short version: NoFi Games works entirely offline. We don't collect your data, we don't track you, and we don't show you ads. Your scores, settings, and favourites are stored on your device and never leave it.

What We Collect

Nothing. NoFi Games does not collect any personal information or usage data. The app functions entirely offline with no network connection required.

The following data is stored locally on your device using IndexedDB:

  • Game scores and high scores
  • App settings and preferences
  • Favourited games

This data never leaves your device. We cannot access it, and it is not transmitted to any server.

How We Use Data

Since we do not collect any data, there is nothing for us to use. The locally stored information listed above exists solely to provide you with a consistent experience between sessions -- your high scores persist, your settings are remembered, and your favourites stay marked.

Third Parties

NoFi Games does not share data with third parties because there is no data to share. The app contains no analytics services, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party tracking of any kind.

Children's Privacy

NoFi Games does not collect personal information from anyone, including children. The app is safe for users of all ages. Because no data is collected or transmitted, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy.

Future Changes

We may introduce optional online features in the future, such as:

  • User accounts (optional sign-up)
  • Online leaderboards

If and when these features are added, they will be opt-in. The app will continue to function fully offline. This privacy policy will be updated before any such features launch, and we will make the changes clear.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
