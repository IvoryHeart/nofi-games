Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

The short version: NoFi.Games works entirely offline. We don't track you and we don't show you ads. Your scores, settings, and favourites are stored on your device. If you choose to opt in, we collect anonymous play statistics (no personal information) to help improve the games.

What's Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device using IndexedDB and localStorage. It never leaves your device unless you opt in to telemetry (see below):

  • Game scores and high scores
  • App settings and preferences
  • Favourited games
  • Saved game state (for resume-where-you-left-off)
  • Daily challenge progress and streak counter
  • A random device identifier (a UUID — not linked to your identity)

Optional Telemetry (Opt-In Only)

If you choose to enable "Help improve games" in Settings, we collect anonymous play statistics to help us balance game difficulty and fix user experience issues. This is off by default and you can turn it off anytime.

What we collect when telemetry is on:

  • Which game was played, at which difficulty, and the score
  • Whether you won or lost, and how long the session lasted
  • Input timing patterns (e.g. average time between moves, pauses longer than 5 seconds)
  • The game's replay log (the sequence of inputs, so we can reproduce the game for analysis)
  • Device class (mobile/tablet/desktop), screen size, timezone, and browser language

What we do NOT collect:

  • Your name, email, IP address, or any personal information
  • Browser fingerprints, advertising IDs, or cross-site tracking
  • Location data (timezone is as specific as it gets)

The device identifier is a random UUID stored in your browser's localStorage. It is not linked to your identity and can be reset by clearing your browser data. We use it solely to group play sessions from the same browser so we can understand play patterns over time.

How We Use the Data

Telemetry data is used exclusively to improve the games:

  • Difficulty balancing: if Easy mode has a very low win rate, we adjust it to be more accessible
  • UX improvement: if players frequently pause or misclick at a specific point, we investigate and fix the design
  • Daily puzzle quality: we analyze which daily seeds produce the most engaging puzzles
  • Anti-cheat: replay logs can be used to verify high scores

We do not sell, share, or monetize this data. It is stored in a database with row-level security — each device's data is isolated from other devices.

Third Parties

Telemetry data is stored in Supabase (a hosted PostgreSQL database). No other third-party services are used. There are no advertising SDKs, no Google Analytics, and no third-party tracking.

Children's Privacy

NoFi Games does not collect personal information from anyone, including children. The app is safe for users of all ages. Because no data is collected or transmitted, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Opt out at any time by turning off "Help improve games" in Settings. Future sessions will not be collected.
  • Reset your device ID by clearing your browser's site data for nofi.games. This severs the link between your past and future sessions.
  • Request deletion by emailing us at privacy@nofi.games. We will delete all data associated with your device ID.

Future Changes

We may introduce optional online features in the future, such as user accounts and online leaderboards. These will be opt-in. The app will continue to function fully offline. This privacy policy will be updated before any such features launch.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
